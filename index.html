Byung-Doh Oh

I'm a Ph.D. student in the Department of Linguistics at The Ohio State University. My work primarily focuses on building computational models of how people resolve ambiguity and construct meaning during sentence processing. I am also interested in natural language processing, especially with regards to semantic representation and parsing.
